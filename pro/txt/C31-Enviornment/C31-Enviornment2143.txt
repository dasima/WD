胶州湾污染物COD的三维扩散与输运研究
闫菊　 鲍献文　 王海　 石磊　 
摘　要：笔者较系统地对环胶州湾工业污染源、生活污染源和海水养殖污染源进行收集和分析，给出影响胶州湾海域环境的主要污染源强的大小及分布；在三维潮流模拟的基础上，建立了一个三维保守污染物COD输运的对流扩散数值模型；模拟所得的COD污染物分布结果与胶州湾的实际监测资料符合良好。
关键词：COD；　三维水质模拟；　胶州湾
分类号：X55　文献标识码：A
文章编号：1001-6929(2001)02-0014-04
Three Dimensional Simulation on Transportation and Diffusion of Pollutant COD in Jiaozhou Bay
YAN Ju（Ocean University of Qingdao,　Qingdao 266003,　China）　
BAO Xian-wen（Ocean University of Qingdao,　Qingdao 266003,　China）　
WANG Hai（Ocean University of Qingdao,　Qingdao 266003,　China）　
SHI Lei（Environmental Protection Bureau of Qingdao,　Qingdao 266001,　China）　

Abstract：Collection and analysis of industrial residential and maricultural pollution sources were systematically carried out for Jiaozhou Bay. The intensity and distribution of major pollution sources for the marine environment were given. On the basis of three-dimensional (3-D) simulation of tidal current, a 3-D model of transportation and diffusion of conservative pollutants was built and applied. The model was verified by observations of COD distribution.
Keywords：COD;　3-D water quality model;　Jiaozhou Bay
