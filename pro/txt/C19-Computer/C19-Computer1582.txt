实际意义下n-D单边矩阵方程有解的充要条件
吕振肃　摆玉龙　年晓红
　　摘要：本文在讨论n-D矩阵方程求解条件的基础上，针对由Agathoklis等人提出的具有实际意义的n维（n-D）离散系统，给出了判定n-D矩阵方程是否有解的充要条件，并给出了判定系统是否具有实际意义稳定性的方法，以算例加以说明．

NECESSARY AND SUFFICIENT SOLVABILITY CONDITIONS OF N-D
UNILATERAL MATRIX EQUATION IN PRACTICAL SENSE
LU Zhensu　BAI Yulong　NIAN Xiaohong
(Department of Electronics and Information Science, Lanzhou University, Lanzhou,730000)
　　Abstract　In the light of n-D discrete systems in the practical sense which was introduced by Agathoklis in 1983,we first discuss the solvable condition of n-D unilateral matrix equations．Simple and constructive necessary and sufficient solvability conditions are derived．A new method to determine the stability of a n-D discrete systems in practical sense is presented． All the obtained result is illustrated by a example of 2-D systems．
　　Key words　n-D systems，matrix equation，practical sense
