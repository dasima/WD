二维时频面的MTI滤波算法研究
唐正军　 刘代志　 
摘　要：分析了传统的动目标显示（MTI）滤波算法的缺陷，提出了一种二维时频面的MTI滤波算法。该方法利用时频分析多通道滤波和时频局域化的特点，在时频平面内进行二维滤波，从而获得良好的杂波抑制能力，且具备较强的自适应性和鲁棒性。最后，根据实验结果给出了此种滤波算法与其他滤波算法的对比效果。
关键词：动目标显示；小波变换；Mallat算法
分类号：TN957.51+3　文献标识码：A

MTI Filtering Algorithm Using Two-Dimension Time-Frequency Plane
TANG Zheng-jun（Xi'an Second Artillery Engineering College, Xi'an Shaanxi 710025， China）　
LIU Dai-zhi（Xi'an Second Artillery Engineering College, Xi'an Shaanxi 710025， China）　

Abstract：In order to overcome some deficiencies of conventional MTI filtering algorithms, a novel approach using two-dimension time-frequency plane is proposed here, which utilizes characteristics of TFP as multi-channel filtering and time-frequency localization to implement two dimensional filtering process on the time-frequency plane on which target back-scattered echoes are expanded, in order to achieve better clutter depression, self-adaptability and robustness. Experimental results are presented at the end of this paper.
Keywords：Moving target indication； Wavelet transform； Mallat algorithm
