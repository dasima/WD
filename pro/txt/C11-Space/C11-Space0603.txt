弹道导弹基于仿真的落点精度修正
熊海林　 陈坚　 沈永福　 邓方林　 
摘　要：介绍了利用计算机仿真技术预测、修正弹道导弹落点，从而提高其命中精度的原理和方法，给出了导弹射前初始状态的测量方法以及导弹仿真的程序模型，并对一枚具体的导弹作了落点的仿真预测与落点修正，仿真的结果与实际情况相吻合。此方法在弹道导弹的实弹演习与作战中具有十分重要的价值，可在相关单位广泛使用。
关键词：弹道导弹；蒙特卡罗法；落点精度；修正；工具误差
分类号：TJ761.3; O242.2　文献标识码：A

Correcting of Fall-Point Precision of Ballistic Missile Based on Simulation
XIONG Hai-lin（Xi'an Second Artillery Engineering College, Xi'an Shaanxi, 710025，China）　
CHEN Jian（Xi'an Second Artillery Engineering College, Xi'an Shaanxi, 710025，China）　
SHEN Yong-fu（Xi'an Second Artillery Engineering College, Xi'an Shaanxi, 710025，China）　
DENG Fang-lin（Xi'an Second Artillery Engineering College, Xi'an Shaanxi, 710025，China）　

Abstract：Introduces a method to forecast and correct ballistic missile fall-point and advance its precision based on computer simulation, presents a method to measure missile's initial states and a program model of missile simulation. In this paper, a example is given, its result is anastomosed with facts. This method has very important values in exercise and operation of ballistic missile, can be widely used in related units.
Keywords：Ballistic missile; Monte-carlo method; Precision of fall-point; Correction; Error of tools
