电解凝絮法处理高盐度有机废水的实验研究
王宏　 郑一新　 钱彪　 宁平　 
摘　要：应用电解凝絮法处理紫胶合成树脂生产过程中排放出的高盐度有机废水实验结果表明，该方法能够有效地去除废水中的有机污染物，提高透明度，CODCr去除率达94%，BOD5去除率达90%以上，并且设备简单、耐冲击性好、操作方便、不需要投加化学药剂，易实现现代化管理。
关键词：高盐度有机废水； 电化学反应； 凝絮； 浮选

The Treatment of High-Salinity Organic Wastewater by Electrocoagulation
WANG Hong（Kunming Environmental Science Institute, Kunming　650032, China）　
ZHENG Yi-xin（Kunming Environmental Science Institute, Kunming　650032, China）　
QIAN Biao（Kunming Environmental Science Institute, Kunming　650032, China）　
NING Ping（Kunming University of Science and Technology, Kunming　650093, China）　

Abstract：The electro-coagulation and floatation method was applied for the treatment of high-salinity organic wastewater of producing synthetic resin by the lac . It was found that CODCr and BOD5 removal rates were 94% and 90% respectively. In addition to its obvious effectiveness of treatment for organic compounds, the electro-coagulation and floatation method requires only simple facilities, with high resistance to impact and simple operations, without additional chemical agents and easy to realize management modernization.
Keywords：high-salinity organic wastewater; electrochemistry; coagulation; floatation
