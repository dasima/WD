煤灰预处理-好氧生物技术处理有机废水的研究
李武　 董有　 夏文林　 
摘　要：介绍了煤灰预处理-好氧生物技术对有机废水的处理研究。结果表明，当有机废水中CODCr为1162mg/L，SS为382mg/L时，该技术对有机废水CODCr的去除率达96.9%，悬浮物的去除率达91.5%，处理废水的运行费用为0.22元/t。
关键词：煤灰；　好氧生物处理；　生物流化床；　有机废水
分类号：X703　文献标识码：A

The study of the Organic Wastewater Treatment Using Coal Ash Pre-Treatment and Aerobic Biological Technology
LI Wu（Wuhan Environmental Protection Research Academy,Ministry of Metallurgical Industry and State Environmental Protection Administration,　Wuhan 430081,China）　
DONG You（Wuhan Environmental Protection Research Academy,Ministry of Metallurgical Industry and State Environmental Protection Administration,　Wuhan 430081,China）　
XIA Wen-lin（Wuhan Environmental Protection Research Academy,Ministry of Metallurgical Industry and State Environmental Protection Administration,　Wuhan 430081,China）　
Abstract：This paper introduces the study of the organic wastewater treatment using coal ash pretreatment and aerobic biological technology.The results show that using the technology,the removal ratio of CODCr of organic wastewater is 96.9%,the removal ratio of SS is 91.5%，and the running fee is 0.22 yuan per ton wastewater,when the draining organic wastewater CODCr and SS concentration is 1162 mg/L and 382 mg/L respectively.
Keywords：coal ash;　aerobic biological treatment;　biological fluidized bed;　organic wastewater
