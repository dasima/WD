生态旅游农业发展初探
朱信凯　雷海章　张娇健
摘　要：分析了从国外生态旅游农业的发展，论述了生态旅游农业形成和发展的社会、经济基础及其他发展的重大意义，并就典型案例分析提出笔者对我国生态旅游农业发展的几点思考。

A Preliminary Discussion on Ecological TouringAgriculture
ZHU Xinkai1　LEI Haizhang1　ZHANG Jiaojian2
(1.College of Economy and Commerce, Huazhong Agricultural University, Wuhan ,430070,China；
2.The State Revenue of Taishan District of Taian City , Taian 271000,China)
Abstract:Beginning with the development of the foreign Ecological TouringAgriculture, this paper discusses the social and economic foundation on which the Ecological Touring Agriculture is formed and developed. Then the great significance of the Ecological TouringAgriculture is explained. At last, some ideas of how to improve it are presented on the base of several typical cases.
