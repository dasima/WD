郑州市TSP尘源粒径及形貌特征分析
孙金陵　 袁思平　 范相阁　 
摘　要：介绍了用扫描电子显微镜(SEM)和电子探针微观分析(EMA)方法对城市尘源的粒径分布及其形貌特征进行分析研究的情况和结果，该方法能清楚地了解尘源样的粒径分布状况形貌特征。
关键词：尘源；　粒径分布；　形貌特征
分类号：X513　文献标识码：A
文章编号：1001-6929(2001)01-0016-03
Analysis of Particle Diameter and Shape and Appearance Characteristic of Dust Sources of TSP in Zhengzhou City
SUN Jing-ling（Zhengzhou Municipal Environmental Protection Monitoring Center,Zhengzhou　450007，China）　
YUAN Si-ping（Zhengzhou Municipal Environmental Protection Monitoring Center,Zhengzhou　450007，China）　
FAN Xiang-ge（Zhengzhou Municipal Environmental Protection Monitoring Center,Zhengzhou　450007，China）　
Abstract：This paper introduces the use of scan electrical microscope (SEM) and electrical-probe micro analysis(EMA) method to analyze and study the distribution of particle diameter of sources of dust and characteristic of its shape and appearance.Through this method we can understand clearly the distribution status of particle diameter of sources of dust and its characteristic of shape and appearance.
Keywords：dust source;　particle diameter distribution;　shape and appearance characteristic
