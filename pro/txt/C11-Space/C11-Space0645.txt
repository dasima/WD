FY-1C卫星的应用与展望
高火山　 李惠增　 
摘　要：介绍了我国独立自主研制的第一代太阳同步轨道气象卫星――FY-1的性能和主要功能，着重介绍了FY-1C星在气象遥感、海洋遥感、空间环境研究、地球环境监测、抗灾救灾和国民经济建设中的应用，以及对国际气象业务的贡献，并展望了我国下一代极轨气象卫星的发展。
关键词：卫星；应用卫星；气象卫星；卫星应用

Application and Prospect for FY-1C Satellite
GAO Huo-shan（SAST,Shanghai 200233, China）　
LI Hui-zeng（No.807 Institute of SAST, Shanghai 200233, C hina）　

Abstract：This paper presents principal performance and function of FY-1 meteorological s atellite which belongs to the first generation of polar orbiting meteorological satellite developped independently and with the initiative in Chinese own hands, and in particular expounds FY-1C meteorological satellite's application in the realm of meteorological and oceanic remote sensing, spatial environment study, global environment monitoring, disaster mitigation and other application in soci al and economic interests, as well as contribution to international meteorologic al operation. Finally, out look for the development of next generation of Chines e polar orbiting meteorological satellite is presented.
