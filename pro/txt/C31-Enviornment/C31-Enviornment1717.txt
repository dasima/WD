满洲里市水体中氟化物的环境特征
严登华　 何岩　 邓伟　 王金达　 
摘　要：满洲里市地表水和地下水中氟化物的含量已超过有关水质标准，在分析氟化物环境表征的基础上，探讨了氟化物在水体中的迁移转化，指出地表水体中氟化物污染主要是流域的非点源污染，地下水中氟化物主要是由于降水淋溶和岩石中含氟岩石的溶解引起的，据此提出相应的治理对策。
关键词：氟化物；　环境表征；　环境行为；　治理；　满洲里市

The Environmental Characteristic of Fluoride in the Water Body of Manzhouli City
YAN Deng-hua（Changchun Institute of Geography,CAS,Changchun 130021,China）　
HE Yan（Personnel Education Bureau,CAS,Beijing 100864,China）　
DENG Wei（Changchun Institute of Geography,CAS,Changchun 130021,China）　
WANG Jin-da（Changchun Institute of Geography,CAS,Changchun 130021,China）　
Abstract：The fluoride in ground and surface water in Manzhouli City is beyond related criteria of water quality.This paper described the transfer and transform of the fluoride in the water body based on analysis of fluoride token,pointed out that the fluoride pollution in the surface water is from non-point pollution sources and the origin of fluoride in ground water is the eluviation of precipitation and the rock containing the fluoride,and gave out the treatment countermeasure.
Keywords：fluoride;　environmental token;　environmental action;　treatment;　Manzhouli City
